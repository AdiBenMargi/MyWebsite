Hapoel Tel Aviv is an Israeli football club from the city of Tel Aviv, which belongs to the Hapoel movement and plays in the Premier League. The club was established in 1923, with the establishment of the Hapoel Tel Aviv Association, and is one of the oldest clubs in Israel, and the founders of the top league in the Land of Israel when it participated in the debut season (1931/1932). The club's colors are red and white, and the team's home field is Bloomfield Stadium located in Jaffa.
